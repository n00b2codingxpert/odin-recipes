Salad

Description

A salad is a perfect way to eat healthy

a yummy summer treat!

Ingredients

  • One bag of spinach
  • 1 head of romaine
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • tomato
  • red onions
  • feta cheese

Directions

  1. wash lettuce
  2. chop tomato
  3. chop red onion
  4. chop feta cheese
  5. mix everything in one large bowl
  6. salt and pepper the top
  7. enjoy!
