Mashed Potato

Description

Supplement your meal with a lovely starch

mashed potatoes go with everything

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of red potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • half cup of sour cream
  • cup of milk

Directions

  1. wash potatoes
  2. bring large pot of water to boil
  3. boil potatoes until a fork slides through them easily
  4. drain pot, begin mashing potatoes with masher
  5. add butter, sour cream, and milk
  6. salt and pepper to taste
  7. once mixture is light and fluffy, you are done
  8. enjoy!
