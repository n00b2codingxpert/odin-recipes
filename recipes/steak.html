Steak

Description

This lovely cut of meat is cooked to perfection

look at those beautiful colors, and it should melt in your mouth

Ingredients

  • 16oz of New York Strip
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 clove of garlic

Directions

  1. heat pan to medium high heat
  2. put butter on pan
  3. season steak with salt and pepper
  4. mince garlic
  5. once butter is melted, add garlic and steak
  6. cook each side about 4 mins
  7. enjoy!
